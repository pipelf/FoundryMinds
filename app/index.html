What is Foundry Minds

This is a group of people with a hunger for creating and solving real problems. Foundry Minds is started as a need for keeping our creative minds active and as a medium to validate potential solutions as fast as possible for problems that are worth solving. Using Human Centered Design techniques, the group is an outlet to be creative and participate in the creation of potential new businesses and social ventures.

This is an opportunity to dive in and learn as a group the concepts of Human Center Design and Design Thinking while applying them to real life needs.

Product concepts that demonstrate traction and have a potential for a viable business model can be moved to the next stage for the creation of a business model and incubation of the product. Those in the group that are willing to move to this stage for a specific validated product will own and lead the effort of product development as founders.

The ultimate goal for Foundry Minds is to become a product design and validation outlet for the Gainesville Startup community and promote the creation of new ventures.

Process

Need Finding

Stage 1

Goal:To gather as many needs from the group members, run them through the “Frequency, Density, Pain” test, and select a need that passes the test and aligns with the team’s interests and expertise.

Map and Inspiration

Stage 2

Goal:To start at the end with a long term goal, turn our assumptions into questions, and create a map to visualize the end goal. In addition we will gather as much information as possible about the area related to the selected need, and analyze all the information gathered in order to be able to recognize opportunity areas that will lead to meaningful insights and a target to focus on.

Lean Canvas

Stage 3

Goal:To understand the value proposition, infrastructure, customers, and finances and viability of the potential solution. To be review after each following stage.
